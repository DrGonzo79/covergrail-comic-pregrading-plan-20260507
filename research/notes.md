## Market/Domain Notes

- CGC grading fees can start around $27–$30 for modern comics and increase for vintage/high-value tiers, with separate fees for imaging, custom labels, prescreen rejects, etc.
- Turnaround estimates are measured in working days and are not guaranteed.
- Grade cliffs matter: a 9.8 vs 9.4 modern key can materially change resale economics.
- Existing visible defect categories include spine stress/ticks, color-breaking creases, bends, dents, corner crunches, stains, foxing, staple wear, miswraps, spine roll, tears, and page quality.
- Important product truth: raking light and guided capture are essential because many high-grade defects are subtle.

## Competitive Notes

- There are early AI comic grading tools and experiments already appearing.
- Differentiation should not be “we guess a grade.” It should be:
  1. guided capture quality,
  2. transparent defect annotation,
  3. economics decisioning,
  4. batch workflows for sellers/shops,
  5. feedback loop from actual grading results.
