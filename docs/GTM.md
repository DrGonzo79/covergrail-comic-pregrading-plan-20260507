# Go-To-Market Plan

## Beachhead

Comic sellers and collectors making repeated grading decisions, not casual readers.

## Channels

1. YouTube/TikTok demonstrations: “Can AI predict my CGC grade?” Use predicted-versus-confirmed results as the recurring trust loop.
2. Reddit/community content: r/comicbookcollecting, r/comicswap, CGC/CBCS forums.
3. Whatnot/eBay seller tooling.
4. Local comic shop batch-intake offer.
5. Pressing/cleaning service partnerships.

## Hook

“Before you spend $50+ and wait weeks, scan it.”

## First Offer

Offer ladder:
- Lead magnet: free comic grading guide.
- Signup hook: 3 free scans.
- Frontend: $1.99 per scan for casual collectors.
- Core: $29/month unlimited predictions.
- Dealer: $499/month for bulk estate and submission-lot workflows.

## Trust Strategy

- Publish blind-test accuracy reports.
- Use grade bands and disclaimers.
- Show side-by-side model prediction vs actual slab results.
- Recruit known collectors/pressers as advisors.

## Content Ideas

- “9.8 dream or 9.2 reality?”
- “The spine tick that cost $400.”
- “Should you press before grading?”
- “AI vs CGC: blind test batch #1.”


## Source Signal Snapshot

- Keyword: `Cgc slabs` — ~4.4K volume, +817% growth.
- GTM score from source analysis: 8/10.
- Strongest communities to test first: Reddit, Facebook collector groups, YouTube grading channels.
